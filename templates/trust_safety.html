Trust & Safety

Your safety and privacy are our top priorities. Learn about our policies and practices.

Adult-Only Content Protection

We take age verification seriously. Adult-only content is strictly gated and only accessible to verified adult users.

Age Verification

  • All users must provide their date of birth during registration
  • Only users who are 18 years or older can access adult-only content
  • Date of birth cannot be changed by users after registration (staff review required)
  • Users can opt-out of viewing adult content in their profile settings

Content Filtering

  • Adult-only categories are clearly marked and restricted
  • Adult-only content is automatically filtered from public galleries
  • Content visibility respects user age and preferences
  • Non-authenticated users never see adult-only content

Staff Oversight

  • Only staff members can mark categories as "adult-only"
  • All date of birth change requests are reviewed by staff
  • Adult content access is blocked during pending DOB change requests

Content Moderation

We maintain a safe and respectful community through active moderation and user reporting.

Reporting Content

If you encounter content that violates our community guidelines, please report it immediately:

  • Use the contact form to report inappropriate content
  • Include the photo ID, user, or category in your report
  • Specify the reason for your report (harassment, inappropriate content, copyright violation, etc.)
  • Reports are reviewed by staff within 1 business day

Staff Moderation

  • Staff members can edit or delete any content on the platform
  • Comments can be removed by photo owners or staff
  • All moderation actions are logged for accountability
  • Users who repeatedly violate guidelines may face account restrictions

Community Guidelines

  • Respect all users and their work
  • No harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory content
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property
  • Do not share personal information without consent
  • Adult content must be properly categorized and marked

Data Privacy & Handling

We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we handle your data.

What We Collect

  • Account information: username, email, display name, and profile picture
  • Date of birth (required for age verification)
  • User role (photographer, model, MUA, or visitor)
  • Content you upload: photos, descriptions, and metadata
  • Interactions: likes, comments, messages, and connections

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To enforce age verification and content policies
  • To facilitate connections and messaging between users
  • To send important account-related communications
  • To respond to support requests and contact form submissions

Data Security

  • All data is stored securely on our servers
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Date of birth information is protected and only used for age verification
  • Staff access to user data is logged and audited
  • We follow industry best practices for data protection

Your Rights

  • You can update your profile information at any time
  • You can delete your account and all associated data
  • You can opt-out of viewing adult content in your settings
  • You can control who sees your photos through visibility settings
  • You can request a copy of your data by contacting us

Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts and content are permanently removed from our systems
  • Audit logs are retained for security and compliance purposes
  • Backup data is securely deleted according to our retention policy

Questions or Concerns?

If you have questions about our Trust & Safety policies or need to report an issue, please don't hesitate to contact us.
